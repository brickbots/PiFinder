# PiFinder Case Accesories

This directory contains a variety of useful accessories.  Unless otherwise
noted these are contributed by Brickbots

## 90 Degree Dovetail

Fits in a standard synta style finder shoe and offers a place to mount
another shoe at 90 degrees to the original.  If you have mounted your
scope side-saddle and the finder shoe is pointing out horizontally, this
may be the part you need to get your PiFinder mounted.

Requires 2x m4 inserts to provide a place to mount a commercial finder shoe

## Screen Cover

Contributed by KAL and found at:
* https://www.printables.com/model/1545603-v3-pifinder-protective-cover/files
* https://www.printables.com/model/1536615-v25-pifinder-protective-cover/files

A screen cover which friction fits onto the front of the PiFinder.  Designed to
be printed in a flexible material and with a version for v2.x and v3 style PiFinders!

## Tube Shoe Mounts

Clamps around a tube and provides a place to mount a finder shoe.  Tube OD size
is indicated in filename

Requires:
* 4x 1/4-20 Bolts
* 4x 1/4-20 Threaded inserts
* 2x M4 Threaded inserts
